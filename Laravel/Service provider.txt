Service provider do ready the services that is needed for applicatio.
Service providers in laravel application is the central place where application is bootstrapped. That is, laravel’s core services and our application’s services, classes and their dependencies are injected in service container through providers.

3 types of services is found in larave
*Core service
*Package services
*Application services

As a example of one package in spatie that is related roles-permission.Service setup spatie package to laravel project by which it can use it.
